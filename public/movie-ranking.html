My favouriate Movies / Mini-series / Web series of all time

Here are my top three list

Chernobyl

Chernobyl is a historical drama television miniseries, produced by HBO and Sky Uk. The series was written by Craig Mazin and directed by Johan Renck. It has a very unique way of narrating the event by showing the adverseries from the persepective of a person who is witnessing it.

Chhello Divas

Chhello Divas – A New Beginning (English: The Last Day – A New Beginning) is a Gujarati coming of age comedy film, written and directed by Krishnadev Yagnik. The movie stars Malhar Thakar, Yash Soni, Mitra Gadhavi, Aarjav Trivedi, Rahul Raval, Janki Bodiwala, Kinjal Rajpriya, and Netri Trivedi. The plot revolves around the lives of eight friends in their last year of college.

Chichore

This movie holds a special place in my heart because it shows how a students suffer in Indian Education system because of a very cut throat competiton. It show cases the pressure and anxiety faced by many students before the result of IIT-JEE the toughest engineering exams in India with an acceptance rate of 1 to 1.5 perectange. Nearly every year 1 to 1.5 million students appear for examination to get into IIT's which can be said as equivalent of MIT. The fun fact is these students keep IVY league universities as their back up if they are not able to get into IIT and there are many examples of it that students failed to get into IIT but was accepted by IVY league universities. I myself have given the exam and I cleared it by securing 45K rank out of over a million student. Just the fact that I have gone through the phase it is very close to my heart and I have read many articles about student commiting suicide because of not getting into IIT which is also addressed in the movie.
